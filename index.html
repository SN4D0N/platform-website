Smart Alerts

Disruptions, before
they ruin your day.

Platform watches your journey and pushes you the moment anything changes — delay, cancellation, or platform move.

  • Notified when your train physically arrives at the platform
  • Delay and cancellation alerts the moment they happen
  • Platform confirmed with your connection buffer included
  • Connection at risk alerts before you miss your change
  • Approaching destination alert 10 minutes before arrival
  • Pre-journey reminder 30 minutes before departure
